Vim
* za to toggle fold, zA toggle fold harder
* zm increase fold, zM fold all (across buf)
* zr reduce fold, zR unfold all (across buf)
* gv to re-select last visual area
* remember you can use shell command to sort, ... things
  e.g. '<,'>!sort
